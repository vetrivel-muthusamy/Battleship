Area of a circle

Given the radius of the circle, you need to find an area of that circle. Use this formula:

\( S = \pi R^2 \)

Where \( S \) is an area of a circle, \( \pi \) is a Math.PI constant, \( R \) is a radius of the circle.


Sample Input:
100
